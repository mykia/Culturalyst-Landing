Culturalyst is a platform that makes it easy for anyone to become a patron of local culture.


For $1/month or more, patrons directly support their favorite artists and culture bearers, in exchange for exclusive access and content. Providing an organized directory of creatives, Culturalyst empowers our city to explore and support its biggest social and economic asset.
